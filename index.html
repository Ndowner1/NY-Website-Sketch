About New York

Hello, and welcome to the site that introduces New York aka, the Big Apple! There are some cool facts to know about New York.

  1. New York was established as a colony in 1664, being one of the original 13 colonies.
  2. New York is home to an estimated 19.5 million people.
  3. It is the core of art, economics, landscape, and diversity, producing notable things such as:
    • The Statue of Liberty
    • Niagara Falls
    • Hip Hop
    • Wall Street

New York's landscape is diverse, having several key regions with differing geographies. The most notable is New York City which exhibits an urbanized landscape defined by skyscrapers and busy streets. However, natural areas and historical landmarks are still preserved, such as Central Park, making the city a unique place to be! As you go further north, you'll see more of it's natural landscapes which can be seen in places like Hudson Valley, the Finger Lakes, Niagara Falls, and the Catskill and Adirondack mountains.

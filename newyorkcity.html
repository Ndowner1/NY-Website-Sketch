New York City is probably the most popular city within the state, known more than the capital, Albany itself. It is home to the Statue of Liberty, one of the most well-known monuments within the nation. New York is also well-known for the diverse food that it offers, as tourists regularly travel here to try the local pizza slices, fried foods, and hot dogs. New York City is made up of five boroughs: Queens, Brooklyn, Manhattan, the Bronx, and Staten Island.

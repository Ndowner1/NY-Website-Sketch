Albany is considered an urban environment due to its infrastructure, high population density, and economic activity. As the capital, it reflects the high urban attributes reflective of the entire state. The average income is stated to be $72,000, as of 2019; however, the median is noted to be significantly lower (estimated to be $53,000). Average incomes, especially in capital cities, can be skewed by high earners who reside in them compared to other cities.
